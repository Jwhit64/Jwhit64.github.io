WMDTech

My time at WMDTech was by far the most beneficial experience I had come across, soon after employment I had become the only software engineer working on the project posted here, and I wanted to see it succeed. So I took lead and taught myself from the ground up how I would make this idea come alive, I wanted results and I wanted things to work properly.

Nearly 3 years later I had achieved my goal, I provided working results from front-end to back-end development I learned the ins and outs of what exactly goes into a precision robotics system and expanded my knowledge and understanding of what exists in the tech world, but more importantly the future that it holds.

However, portions of my time were also spent doing training as an instructor for consumers of WMDTech, I had taught multiple microcontroller courses around the world all of which helped solidify my understanding in programming and basic electrical theory.
